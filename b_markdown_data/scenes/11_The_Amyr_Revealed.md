# The Amyr Revealed

## Name
The Amyr Revealed

## Part
Part One: The Return

## Chapter
11-13

## What happens in this scene
- Kvothe confronts Master Lorren about the systematically missing texts
- Lorren's usual placidity breaks—something harder shows through
- That night, Kvothe is summoned to a hidden chamber beneath the Archives
- He meets the Amyr: not disbanded, but hidden for centuries
- Lorren is among them; others are ancient, some not entirely human
- They reveal the truth:
  - The Church's "Order Amyr" was a pale imitation
  - The original Amyr work to contain something worse than the Chandrian
  - Kvothe's research threatens the balance they've maintained
  - His Lackless heritage makes him especially dangerous
- The ultimatum: stop searching, submit to oversight, or be eliminated
- Kvothe refuses
- He understands: he is not the hunter. He is the hunted.

## Characters present
Kvothe, Master Lorren, The Amyr (multiple members)
