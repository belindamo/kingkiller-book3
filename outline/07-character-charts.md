## KVOTHE

### Basic Information
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Full Name** | Kvothe, son of Arliden and Laurian (Netalia Lackless) |
| **Aliases** | Kote, Kvothe the Bloodless, Kvothe the Arcane, Kvothe Kingkiller, E'lir, Re'lar, Dulator, Maedre |
| **Age** | ~17-19 (story) / ~25 (frame) |
| **Birthdate** | Unknown; raised among Edema Ruh troupers |
| **Physical Description** | Red hair, green eyes, lean build, musician's hands |
| **Voice/Speech** | Can modulate his voice precisely; Aturan with no accent |

### Background
| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Birthplace** | On the road with his troupe |
| **Family** | Parents murdered by Chandrian; only child; heir to Lackless |
| **Education** | Self-taught, then University |
| **Social Class** | Edema Ruh (wandering performers, despised); secretly noble |
| **Formative Event** | Witnessing his entire troupe murdered by the Chandrian |

### Psychology
| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Core Trait** | Brilliance combined with dangerous certainty |
| **Motivation (Abstract)** | To understand; to never be powerless again |
| **Goal (Concrete)** | Destroy the Chandrian; learn all names of power |
| **Deepest Fear** | Being helpless while those he loves suffer |
| **Greatest Flaw** | Arrogance; inability to let things go; seeing people as puzzles |
| **Secret** | He blames himself for his parents' deaths (he was gathering sage) |

### Character Arc (Book 3)
| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Starting Point** | Returning to University with knowledge and confidence |
| **Turning Point 1** | Discovering the Amyr see him as a threat, not an ally |
| **Turning Point 2** | Losing Denna because he couldn't see her as autonomous |
| **Turning Point 3** | Realizing he's been manipulated to open the door |
| **Low Point** | Killing King Roderic; becoming the Kingkiller |
| **Epiphany** | Understanding he's not the hero—he's the weapon |
| **Resolution** | Locking away himself; becoming Kote |

### Relationships
| Character | Relationship | Dynamic |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| **Denna** | Love interest | Tragic; mutual obsession that destroys them |
| **Bast** | Student/keeper | Protective; Bast as guardian of broken Kvothe |
| **Elodin** | Teacher | Respects but doesn't trust; Elodin sees his own past |
| **Simmon/Wilem** | Friends | Genuine friendship; Kvothe puts them in danger |
| **Cinder** | Enemy | The face of the Chandrian for Kvothe |
| **Denna's Patron** | Enemy | Personal—used the woman he loves |

### Skills & Abilities
- **Naming:** Can call the wind; progressing to other names
- **Sympathy:** Exceptionally skilled; can fight with it
- **Artificing:** Creates powerful magical items
- **Music:** Perhaps the most gifted musician alive
- **Combat:** Trained in the Ketan; skilled with sword
- **Languages:** Speaks multiple languages fluently
- **Alchemy:** Competent but not exceptional
- **Rhetoric/Acting:** Can manipulate, deceive, perform

## MASTER ELODIN

### Basic Information
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Full Name** | Elodin |
| **Title** | Master Namer |
| **Age** | Uncertain; appears 30s-40s |
| **Physical Description** | Wild-eyed, often barefoot, unpredictable |

### Psychology
| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Core Trait** | Brilliant madness; sees patterns others miss |
| **Motivation** | Preserve dangerous knowledge; find worthy students |
| **Secret** | He was once where Kvothe is; he barely survived |

### Role in Book 3
Elodin serves as a tragic Cassandra—he can see where Kvothe is heading but cannot stop him without breaking the very rules of Naming that he teaches. His attempts to slow Kvothe down fail because Kvothe's impatience mirrors his own younger self.

## CINDER (FERULE)

### Basic Information
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Full Name** | Ferule (true name); Cinder (common name) |
| **Role** | One of the seven Chandrian |
| **Age** | ~7,000+ years |
| **Physical Description** | White hair, black eyes, moves with fluid grace |
| **Sign** | "Chill and dark of eye" — his presence brings cold |

### Psychology
| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Core Trait** | Weariness masked by cruelty |
| **Motivation** | End the curse; rewrite history |
| **Goal** | Use Denna's song to rehabilitate Chandrian image |
| **Relationship to Kvothe** | Created him by killing his parents; fears what he's become |

### Role in Book 3
Cinder is the personal face of the Chandrian for Kvothe. As Denna's patron, he represents both Kvothe's enemy and the barrier between Kvothe and the woman he loves. His manipulation of Denna is what drives the central tragedy.

## THE AMYR (Collective)

### Information
| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Pre-human; original Amyr predated the Church |
| **Motto** | "For the greater good" |
| **Members** | Include Master Lorren; others in positions of power |
| **Goal** | Contain something worse than the Chandrian |
| **Relationship to Kvothe** | See him as a threat that must be controlled |

### Role in Book 3
The Amyr serve as unexpected antagonists—Kvothe has sought them as allies, only to discover they view him as the problem. Their "greater good" philosophy makes them morally complex: they're not wrong about the danger Kvothe represents, but their methods are oppressive.
